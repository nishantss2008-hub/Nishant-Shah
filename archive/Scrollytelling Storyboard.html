One Continuous Dawn

A single unbroken golden-hour rise — from your desk at first light, out the window, over the neighborhood, above the clouds, into the sun. One breath upward toward the light. Below is the scroll journey, scene by scene, with the camera move and the text-safe zone marked on each frame.

How it stays seamless (and performant)

  • Light does the stitching: every clip ends on the brightness the next one begins on — a window flare, a cloud white-out — so a 0.5s cross-fade hides each seam.
  • Scroll-scrubbed, not autoplayed: clip time maps to scroll position, so it feels truly scroll-driven and never janks.
  • 5 short clips (≤8s, ≤1080p), lazy-loaded, only the active scene decodes; poster frames prevent black gaps. ~15–20MB total.
  • Permanent dark text-safe zone, lower-left (dashed box on each frame) keeps headlines legible over moving footage.
  • Scene 1 IS the 360° room — you arrive at the desk and the bedroom tour's hotspots live right there.
