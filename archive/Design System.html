Reverse-engineered from units.school — its near-black canvas, royal-blue conviction, heavy grotesque headlines, and editorial restraint. These are the tokens and components the portfolio is built from.

Assumption

Dark is the primary surface, light is a full peer (you asked for a toggle). Every token is defined twice.

Decision

One accent — royal blue #2563EB — carries all emphasis. Green & amber are reserved for status & ratings only.

05

Motion principles

Reveal on scroll

Sections rise 22px and fade over 600ms on the --ease-out curve as they enter the viewport. Respects reduced-motion.

Hover lift

Cards translate -4px and gain elevation over 320ms. Arrows nudge +3px. Quick, never bouncy.

Theme cross-fade

Background and text transition over 320ms so the dark↔light switch feels deliberate, not jarring.
